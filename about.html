About Roadbuy

What is it!!

Roadbuy is indian startup company which was created by S.Arvind,who was an electronics and communication engineer born on 20th may 2003. This was the first website made by him and he claimed this Roadbuyas a prototype for his upcoming projects. This Roadbuy is a online shopping platform which sells a limited amount of product that is available in the nearest locations of customer,unlike the other shopping platforms this Roadbuy ,with the access of the location serivices connects the nearest point of the shop.
